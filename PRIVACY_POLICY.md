# Privacy Policy

**Extension:** Remove YouTube Affiliate Overlay  
**Last updated:** June 19, 2026

## Summary

This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any user data.

## Data Collection

This extension collects **no personal data** of any kind. Specifically:

- No browsing history is recorded or transmitted
- No YouTube account information is accessed
- No cookies, identifiers, or fingerprints are collected
- No analytics or telemetry is sent to any server

## Local Storage

The extension uses `chrome.storage.local` exclusively to store metadata about removed affiliate sticker elements (thumbnail image URL, affiliate link, page title, and timestamp). This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is never transmitted to any external server
- Is controlled entirely by you and can be cleared at any time via the extension popup

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not communicate with any third-party service, API, or server. It operates entirely within your browser.

## Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `storage` | Store removed sticker metadata locally on your device |
| `tabs` | Open affiliate links in a new tab when clicked in the popup |

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published in this repository.
